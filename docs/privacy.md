# Privacy Policy

**Espresso Shot Timer**  
Last updated: December 21, 2024

---

## Overview

Espresso Shot Timer is a local app. **All data stays on your device.** We respect your privacy and have designed this app to work completely without collecting any personal information.

---

## No Data Collection

We don't collect any personal data. The app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

- ❌ No personal information collected
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No tracking
- ❌ No third-party data sharing

---

## Sensor Data

The app uses your device's **accelerometer** to detect vibrations from your espresso machine. This sensor data is:

- ✅ Processed locally on your device
- ✅ Never transmitted to any server
- ✅ Never stored beyond the current session

---

## Local Storage

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Calibration settings** – to remember your machine's vibration sensitivity
- **Last shot time** – to display your previous extraction time
- **App preferences** – such as debug mode settings

This data can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Reset Onboarding" feature.

---

## In-App Purchases

If you choose to support the app through in-app purchases, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses **RevenueCat** for managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data related to purchases. For more information, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
